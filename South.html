Uttapam

Uttapam is a dish very similar to the dosa and is a speciality of Tamil Nadu food. It is made by grinding the Urad Dal and rice powder together.

Puttu And Kadala Curry

Puttu and Kandala curry is enjoyed by people of Kerala which is considered to be one of the popular traditional Kerala dishes.

Andhra Pepper Chicken

Andhra pepper chicken is made by marinating chicken pieces in a special mix of turmeric, ginger-garlic paste, green chillies, and lemon juice and is cooked until tender and juicy.
