Lots To Eat

Khandvi

Khandvi is one of the most likable Gujarati breakfast recipes a person can have. Another name given to it in Marathi is ‘Suralichya vadya’.

Prawn balchao

Served as an accompaniment to a rice dish, or spread on toast, this is a spice-infused prawn pickle comprised of a fiery tomato and chilli sauce.

Poha

Poha is not only famous in Madhya Pradesh but famous in all over India. Poha is also known as potato poha. It is served with sliced onion and sev.Jalebi is also served with Poha.

Dal baati churma

This is the classic signature dish.Baati is hard,unleavened bread cooked in the desert areas of Rajasthan plus it requires hardly any water for its preparation.

Pav Bhaji

Amchi Maharashtra food is incomplete without this dish which has come to be synonymous to the state. This lip-smacking dish has won the heart of every Indian.

Muthia

Muthia are dumplings cooked in the traditional style of the Chhattisgarh state. Muthia is prepared with the rice batter which is seasoned with various spices.

Haalbai

Haalbai is a famous sweet from Karnataka that is made from rice, coconut and jaggery. Coconut milk is also added to give a distinct coconut flavour.
